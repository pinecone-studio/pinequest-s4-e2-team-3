# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Travelers visiting Mongolia (primarily Ulaanbaatar) who want real-time, adaptive guidance without relying on an internet-heavy, review-heavy platform. Users are on-the-go, often in unfamiliar territory, sometimes in low-connectivity or high-stress situations (language barrier, navigation, emergencies). They need a companion, not a catalog.

## Product Purpose

Polaris is an AI-powered travel companion for Mongolia. It reshapes the day around conditions — weather, crowds, how the traveler feels — rather than serving a static itinerary. Features: AI guide chat, live voice narration, adaptive journey planning, phrase translation, explore/map, and an SOS emergency flow. Success looks like the traveler feeling like they have a local friend with them.

## Brand Personality

Adventurous · Sharp · Local. Knowledgeable without being corporate. Warm but never anxious. The guide who knows the back streets, not the PR-polished tourism brochure.

## Anti-references

- Generic travel apps (TripAdvisor, Booking.com) — avoid busy photo-grids, star ratings everywhere, review counts as the primary signal
- AI chatbot wrappers — avoid leading with "ask me anything" as the primary UI metaphor; the destination and experience come first

## Design Principles

1. **The place, not the platform** — UI recedes so Mongolia stays in focus; chrome is a tool, not a statement
2. **Calm confidence** — never alarmist (even in the SOS flow), never cheerleader-loud; measured, clear, useful
3. **Local texture** — warmth and personality come from content, copy, and typography, not decoration
4. **Adaptive, not prescriptive** — the interface responds to context (time of day, status, location) rather than imposing a rigid structure
5. **Earn every pixel** — no card, shadow, or visual element without a functional reason

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. prefers-reduced-motion respected on all custom animations. Focus rings visible and on-brand. Semantic HTML throughout. Emergency (SOS) flow must be operable under stress — large tap targets, clear language, no cognitive load.
